## Context (stated prior)

Going in, I lean cautiously yes. The US labor market has been more resilient than the cooling narrative all cycle, and May printed +172k -- above the line. But a clear negative-revision trend and summer seasonal softness make this a genuine coin-flip, not a gimme. That is exactly why it is the right kind of fast-resolving public call to anchor: real uncertainty, a hard date, a source nobody controls.

## Decision

Pre-register a board call that the June 2026 US nonfarm payrolls report lands at or above +150,000, resolving against the BLS Employment Situation release on 2026-07-02. File it as bet #1 of a public, recurring forecasting cadence -- the board keeps score in public, before the fact.
